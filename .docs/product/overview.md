# Product Overview

## Problem

Picking the wrong tenant is one of the most expensive mistakes a small landlord can
make — missed rent, evictions, property damage, legal cost. Yet for a DIY landlord
with a handful of units, screening today is **slow and manual**: collecting documents
over email and text, chasing references by phone, and eyeballing pay stubs with no
consistent way to compare applicants. It's anxiety-inducing and error-prone.

## Solution

dwellow turns screening into a fast, structured, repeatable process:

- The landlord builds a **custom application form** for a unit and shares a **link**.
- Applicants self-serve — fill it out, upload supporting documents, list references.
- dwellow **contacts references** automatically and an **AI scoring job** evaluates
  each submission against the landlord's criteria.
- The landlord sees every applicant side-by-side with an **AI-generated score** on a
  single dashboard and makes a confident decision quickly.

> The goal: cut screening time dramatically and reduce manual effort **while still
> catching the issues and red flags a busy landlord would otherwise miss.**

## Who it's for

Primary: **DIY small landlords** (1–20 units) who self-manage and are price-sensitive,
valuing ease-of-use over depth. See [personas](./personas.md).

## Why now / why us

- Screening is the **highest-anxiety moment** in a landlord's job — a strong wedge.
- By collecting **tenant-provided documents** instead of pulling regulated bureau
  reports, v1 avoids the biggest barrier in the space (becoming a regulated Consumer
  Reporting Agency) and ships far faster. See
  [ADR 0002](../decisions/0002-no-bureau-integrations.md).

## North star

Screening is the **front door**. The long-term vision is to become the small
landlord's all-in-one operating system: screening → lease & e-sign → rent collection
→ maintenance → accounting. See [roadmap](../roadmap.md).
